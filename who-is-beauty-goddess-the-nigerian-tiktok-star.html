    Beauty Goddess (Real Name: Merry John, Born: 4th May 2001, Age: 22 years old) is a Nigerian-Ghanaian TikTok star, content creator, social media influencer, and brand ambassador. The 22-year-old celebrity has won a few awards for her contribution to TikTok. She won the Pulse TikTok Influencer of the Year award in 2021.

    Beauty Goddess shot to fame for sharing lip-syncs, dances, and comedy-related clips on TikTok. She later added Instagram, where she posts beauty, fashion, and lifestyle pictures. Beauty Goddess has now grown very popular and is an ambassador to many brands.

    What is Beauty Goddess’ Real Name?

    Beauty Goddess’ real name is Merry John. She uses Beauty Goddess on her TikTok, Instagram, and other social media accounts. Beauty was born on 4th May 2001 to Nigerian and Ghanaian parents, Mr. and Mrs. John. She is of Nigerian-Ghanaian blood, and she is based mainly in Lagos, Nigeria.

    She has 3 siblings, two older brothers, and a younger sister. News had it that she lost her younger sister to the cold hands of death in October 2022. However, she is the 3rd child out of four children. Beauty is from an average home, and had to work hard to make herself known as we have today.

    The star admitted in a video that she is not in the university yet but has plans to further her education in the future. She also stressed the fact that her parents would not be happy with her if she didn’t further her education.

    Beauty Goddess is a TikTok, Instagram, and YouTube Star

    Beauty Goddess is one of those celebrities (like Janemena etc.) who has the internet and social media as their workspace. Whatever business she does to make a living is in the internet space. As mentioned, the star is famous for the lip-syncs, dances, and comedy-related clips she posts on TikTok.

    This transformed her from a nobody to the celebrity status she can boast of today. It was her presence and contributions on TikTok that bought her the fame she enjoys today, as she has over 3.5M followers on the platform. It also brought money into her pocket as she earns from the platform.

    Beauty Goddess has also worked with some fashion and beauty brands which include Trim & Fit Diet Coffee Factory, Nekky Hairs, and Glowsbyniffy. She promotes these brands on her Instagram page, where she has over 572k followers. She now has some endorsement deals and uses her social media accounts to promote these brands.

    In addition, Beauty Goddess also has a YouTube channel with over 41K subscribers and thousands of viewers for each post. The channel was created in December 2020 with content encompassing fashion, beauty, and lifestyle vlogs.

    Are Softmadeit and Beauty Goddess Dating?

    Beauty Goddess and Softmadeit (Jerry Chuks) have been rumored to be in a relationship. Beauty and Soft have been spotted several times in TikTok videos together. Because of this, fans have concluded that the duo is in a romantic relationship with each other.

    Also, Softmadeit used to feature different girls in his videos. This made his fans see him as promiscuous. In a bid to change how his fans see him, Soft decided to stick to one woman, Beauty Goddess. This, again, has sparked rumors that they are in a relationship.

    However, Beauty Goddess and Softmadeit have debunked the rumors in TikTok and YouTube videos. They maintain that they are not dating and that they only appear in videos and song promos together because their fans, as well as the musicians they promote their songs, yearn for such.

    What Happened Between Softmadeit and Beauty Goddess?

    Beauty has denied being in a relationship with Softmadeit several times. She has maintained that nothing happened between them. According to her, they are really close but were never dating. As such, they did not break up because they were never in a relationship at first.

    Soft has also denied ever dating Softmadeit. In fact, Beauty is not interested in a love relationship at the moment; but agrees that she is going to get married when the time is right.
