    Lauv Joins Ed Sheeran on North American Stadium Tour

    28 Aug 2024, 00:00

    Get ready to be a little more emotional this upcoming fall – Lauv will be bringing the blue and some new songs to the second half of Ed Sheeran’s North American leg of the Divide tour!

    He’s no stranger to touring with Ed Sheeran, having played his first ever arena shows as the opening act on the Asian leg of the Divide tour last fall.

    https://www.instagram.com/p/BbXO3BmBWvk/

    Since then, he’s gone on his own I Met You When I Was 18. World Tour with shows in Los Angeles, New York City, Toronto, and London sold out. His song “I Like Me Better” has reached 3x Platinum in Australia, and he’s set to release the final two songs of I Met You When I Was 18 on May 31st. Fans attending the Divide tour will have plenty of time to get those lyrics down for the show!

    You can find out more information on tour dates here, and more about Lauv in our Artist 101 series!
