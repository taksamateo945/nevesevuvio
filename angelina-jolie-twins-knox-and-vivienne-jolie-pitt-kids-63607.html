    What a fun shopping day with mom!

    On Sunday, July 19, Angelina Jolie took her twins Knox and Vivienne to Barnes & Noble in LA to buy them gifts for their seventh birthday — and the youngest Jolie-Pitt kids look more grown-up than ever!

    Knox, Angelina and Vivienne on Sunday, July 19.
