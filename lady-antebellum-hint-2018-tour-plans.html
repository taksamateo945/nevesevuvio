    Last year saw the US country music trio Lady Antebellum return from a two-year hiatus with the massive number 1 album ‘Heart Break‘. During their impressive comeback the multi Grammy award winning band consisting of Hillary Scott, Dave Haywood and Charles Kelley also embarked on an extensive world tour. The You Look Good tour was a huge success which saw the band performing to sell-out crowds all across the world.

    While on the road it was confirmed that two of Lady Antebellum’s members were expecting babies. Dave Haywood and his wife  Kelli Cashiola welcomed a beautiful baby girl into the world on December 22nd. The bundle of joy is named Lillie Renee Haywood and is the couples second child and a little sister to adorable Cash.

    While Hillary Scott spent much of the tour cradling a precious baby bump after it was announced that she was carrying twin girls. Hillary and husband Chris Tyrell will welcome their baby girls into the world this February. The couple are already proud parents to the super sweet  Eisele Kaye Tyrrell.

    But while 2018 is set to be an incredibly busy time for Lady Antebellum with their growing families to care for, the trio look all set to hit the road once again this year.

    The exciting news that fans of the country superstars could be seeing them live again this year was announced by the band on social media after they asked supporters where they should tour in 2018.
