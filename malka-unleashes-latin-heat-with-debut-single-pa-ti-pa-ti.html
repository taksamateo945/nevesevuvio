    Malka Unleashes Latin Heat with Debut Single 'PA TI PA TI'

    02 Apr 2024, 00:00

    Get ready to move, groove, and immerse yourself in the vibrant world of Malka, the Latin-infused songstress who is about to set the music scene ablaze with her debut single ‘PA TI PA TI’. It’s a moment long-awaited for the singer-songwriter, and she’s gearing up to showcase exactly what she’s made of.

    Malka’s music is a tight fusion of Reggaeton and Latin-infused dance-pop, a unique and striking sound that she’s expertly crafted across this song. ‘PA TI PA TI,’ translating to “For You For You,” kicks off with a feel-good factor that’s infectious from the start.

    “I wanted my first release to be sexy, playful and fun with good energy as I feel that’s what most of my music is about,” Malka says about her single. “I don’t want my music to be complicated or hard to get, I just want people to push play and dance and feel good. Sure, the topic is about guys always pulling different moves on girls, but it happens, sometimes the other way around but it’s all fun and games!”

    Accompanying the single is an equally sultry music video, shot in Latvia by the talented female director Svetlana Licova. The visuals perfectly complement the energetic and lively vibe of Malka’s music, adding another layer to the overall experience. You can catch the official video [here](insert link).

    Leading up to this groundbreaking release, Malka has been hard at work in the studio, collaborating with some of the best in the business. Teaming up with the UK’s hottest Latin producer DUKUS and recording in Amsterdam with the renowned Pascal Nanlohy, Malka has surrounded herself with top-tier talent to bring ‘PA TI PA TI’ to life.

    Malka is here to make an impact, and ‘PA TI PA TI’ is just the beginning of what promises to be an exciting journey filled with catchy beats, empowering lyrics, and infectious energy.
