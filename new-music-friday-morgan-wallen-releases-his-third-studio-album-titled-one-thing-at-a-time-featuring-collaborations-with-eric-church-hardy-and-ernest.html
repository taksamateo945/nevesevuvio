    Breaking streaming record after streaming record, Morgan Wallen is the ULTIMATE country star of our generation!

    Today (March 3rd), the chart-topping superstar dropped arguably the most highly-anticipated project of 2023, his third studio album titled One Thing At A Time — It is absolutely incredible!

    Consisting of a whopping 36 tracks, fans got to hear a handful of songs prior to the release of the project, including “One Thing At A Time,” “Days That End In Why” and “Tennessee Fan” under the One Thing At A Time Sampler, as well as “Last Night,” “Everything I Love” and “I Wrote The Book” under the 3 Songs At A Time Sampler.

    With these songs aside, one of the most notable tracks on the album is Wallen’s smash hit “You Proof,” which had a ten-week run at the top of the Country Airplay chart, ultimately the shattering the record for the longest-running number one single in that chart’s history.

    To make the tracklist even better, One Thing At A Time features THREE collaborations with A-list celebs as well. Eric Church hopped on the song “Man Made A Bar,” HARDY on “In The Bible” and ERNEST on “Cowgirls,” and all three songs are DEFINITELY soon-to-be hits.

    Maybe we will even be able to hear HARDY perform “In The Bible” and ERNEST perform “Cowgirls” onstage with Wallen during the One Night At A Time world tour… Who knows 👀

    The highly-anticipated tour kicks off on March 15th in Auckland, New Zealand and will run until October 7th in Tacoma, Washington, featuring special guests HARDY, ERNEST, Parker McCollum and Bailey Zimmerman — We seriously can’t wait!

    Aside from Morgan Wallen’s phenomenal new album, there were some singles featured on this week’s New Music Friday playlist that are equally as incredible.

    Jimmie Allen covered “Miss You,” an ICONIC song by The Rolling Stones in honor of the band’s 60th anniversary, while country stars like Ingrid Andress came out with “Runnin (feat. JP Saxe),” Tyler Rich with “I Know You Do,” Priscilla Block with “Couple Spring Breaks Back,” Travis Denning with “Strawberry Wine And A Cheap Six Pack,” Alana Springsteen with “shoulder to cry on,” Brandon Lay with “Like This Like That” and more.

    Television personalities Logan Crosby, a finalist on the hit ABC show Claim To Fame, and Charles Esten, more commonly known as Ward Cameron from the viral Netflix series Outer Banks, released hot new tracks as well. Today, Crosby dropped “After All” and Esten dropped “One Good Move” on all music streaming platforms, and we are absolutely obsessed — Name one thing these guys CAN’T do!
