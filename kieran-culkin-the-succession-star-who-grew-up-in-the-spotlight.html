    Kieran Culkin: The Succession Star Who Grew Up in the Spotlight

    Kieran Culkin is one of the most talented and versatile actors of his generation.

    He has been acting since he was a child, following in the footsteps of his famous brother Macaulay Culkin.

    He has starred in films like Home Alone, Father of the Bride, The Cider House Rules, and Igby Goes Down.

    Also, he has made a name for himself on television, especially as the witty and ruthless Roman Roy on the hit HBO series Succession.

    But who is Kieran Culkin behind the scenes? How did he cope with growing up in a chaotic family?

    What is his personal life like? And what are his plans for the future?

    In this article, we will explore the life and career of Kieran Culkin, the Succession star who grew up in the spotlight.

    Early Life and Family

    Kieran Kyle Culkin was born on September 30, 1982, in New York City.

    He is the son of Kit Culkin, a former Broadway stage actor, and Patricia Brentrup, a telephone operator.

    He has six siblings: Shane, Dakota, Macaulay, Quinn, Christian, and Rory.

    All of them are actors, except for Dakota, who died in a car accident in 2008.

    Kieran also has a paternal aunt, Bonnie Bedelia, who is an actress.

    Kieran spent his early life in the Yorkville neighborhood of Manhattan, where he lived with his parents and siblings.

    His family struggled financially, and his father was often abusive and controlling.

    He pushed his children to pursue acting careers and took a large percentage of their earnings.

    In 1995, his parents separated, and Kieran has been estranged from his father ever since.

    Personal Life and Future Projects

    Kieran married Jazz Charton, a former model, on June 8, 2013.

    They have two children: a daughter named Kinsey Sioux, born in September 2019, and a son named Wilder Wolf, born in August 2021.

    They live in New York City and keep a low profile. Kieran has said that he is very happy with his family and that he enjoys being a father.

    Kieran is currently working on the fourth season of Succession, which is expected to premiere in 2024.

    He has also completed filming a comedy film called A Real Pain, in which he plays a doctor who suffers from chronic pain.

    He has also expressed interest in doing more theater work, as he made his Broadway debut in 2014 in the play This Is Our Youth.

    Kieran Culkin is a successful and respected actor, who has proven his versatility and talent in various genres and mediums.
