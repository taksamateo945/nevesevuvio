    16 Feb 2024, 00:00

    The Bold and The Beautiful spoilers and updates tease Deacon Sharpe’s (Sean Kanan) news will first cause John “Finn” Finnegan’s (Tanner Novlan) disbelief!

    Coming Up On The Bold and The Beautiful

    No one else will believe Deacon either, at least not at first, but later Steffy Forrester (Jacqueline MacInnes Wood) will be shocked!

    TEN TOES! TEN TOES!

    Deacon was so brokenhearted over losing Sheila Carter (Kimberlin Brown) permanently, as everyone else also supposed, he stayed to see her cremation.

    He loved her so much he didn’t want her to be alone even though all that was left was a lifeless corpse-even he believed it was her.

    When he lifted the cover over her head, he kissed the face and told who he thought was Sheila she was loved, after placing flowers.

    But Deacon was in for a shock, as was the cremation director who couldn’t stop the furnace to stop the cremation. As she went down into the flames in a large cardboard box, the force of the flames revealed her feet, and there were TEN TOES!

    Everyone Tells Deacon Sharpe He’s Lost His Mind

    Deacon will tell his horror story to anyone who’ll listen, first of all Sheila’s birth son Finn-who’ll think he lost his mind. Steffy’s had enough, feeling when Deputy Chief Bradley Baker (Dan Martin) gives her the death certificate Sheila’s no longer a worry.

    By the end of the week, Ridge Forrester (Thorsten Kaye) will give Deacon a great big reality check after a tormented Deacon tells him what he saw.

    Steffy will likely not be told anything except to believe what Chief Baker gave her, but by the end of the week Finn will throw Steffy a curveball.

    Finn might admit to Steffy the service gave him closure, but he won’t tell her what Deacon told him right away-at the end of the week it looks like he must.

    Hope Logan Out Of The Loop?

    For some reason, Bold and Beautiful spoilers don’t show Hope Logan (Annika Noelle) reacting to her father’s desperate story about a lookalike being cremated.

    Perhaps Finn is behind that, not wanting her to be troubled about that on top of being dumped by Thomas Forrester (Matthew Atkinson).

    It seems clear that both Ridge and Finn are trying to protect Steffy from hearing Deacon’s desperate stories, so it makes sense that Hope’s left out of the loop as well.

    However, Finn may be given reason to believe himself that the corpse that was cremated had ten toes-perhaps this one’s toe prints don’t match his mother’s.

    The death certificate may include fingerprints or toe prints taken from the corpse that don’t match the original report from when Sheila was supposedly killed by a bear-that’ll be the curveball!
