    You know we love Jennifer Lawrence at CelebrityToob. She is just gorgeous, out-there, and seems like a lot of fun. Like she doesn’t take the world she is in too seriously.  So it makes us happy when we hear good things happen to her. For example, anytime Jennifer Lawrence wins something, we stand up and cheer. We think she deserves it. In this case, Jennifer Lawrence wins AP’s Entertainer of the Year. To that, we all say huzzah!

    As Fox News reported,  Jennifer Lawrence wins the Associated Press’s Entertainer of the Year. The irony is she just barely beat out Miley Cyrus. Man, we would say our feelings on that,  but it would only get us into trouble. Truthfully, Jennifer Lawrence really has been one of the most entertaining people of the year. From the new Hunger Games to American Hustle, she has just owned the latter part of the year.

    On top of that, she is hilarious and genuine in interviews, and just seems like an overall awesome person. Being stunning doesn’t really hurt, either. With 2013 being such a huge year for the young actress, we can’t wait to see what 2014 has in store for her.

    Either way, when Jennifer Lawrence wins, we all win.
